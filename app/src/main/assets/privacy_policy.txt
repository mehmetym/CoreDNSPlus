CoreDNS+ Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 24, 2026

CoreDNS+ is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we treat your information.

1. Zero Data Collection Policy
We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal identifiable information (PII). We do not monitor or log the websites you visit. All DNS filtering happens locally on your device.

2. Local Processing
CoreDNS+ creates a local VPN tunnel to intercept DNS queries. These queries are compared against your local blocklists. None of this data is ever sent to our servers.

3. Permissions
- VPN Service: Necessary for local DNS interception.
- Network Access: Required to resolve allowed queries.
- Storage Access: Used only for importing custom lists.
- Notifications: Used to show service status.

4. No Third-Party Analytics
CoreDNS+ contains no tracking SDKs or ads. We do not share data with third parties.

5. DNS Providers
Your allowed queries are resolved by your chosen provider (e.g., Google, Cloudflare). Check their respective privacy policies.

6. Security
Your data stays on your device. This is the highest level of security possible for a DNS filter.
